# Contributing

This repository is a demo for Atlas. It is meant to show one way to build a public-company research workflow with vertical sources, model routing, and cited reports.

We are not planning ongoing support, feature development, or a long-term maintenance roadmap for this repo. Issues and pull requests are welcome when they fix a clear bug, improve the example, or keep it working with current dependencies, but we may not respond quickly and may close requests that turn the demo into a supported product.

For production use, treat this repo as a starting point and adapt it to your own sources, prompts, models, compliance needs, and operational standards.
